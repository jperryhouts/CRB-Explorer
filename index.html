* Left mouse: Drag point
    A or B around
* Right mouse: Drag both
    A and B around.

* Up|Down: Translate
  forward/back
* Left|Right: Rotate view
  box around its midpoint
* Alt+{Arrow Key}:
  Translate view box

* A/a: Adjust thickness
  of the view box
* Ctrl+{Up|Down}: Change
  the view angle (pitch)
