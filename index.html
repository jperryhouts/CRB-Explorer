On Map
 Mouse clicks:
  * Left: Drag point A *or* B
  * Right: Drag both A *and* B
 Arrow keys:
  * U/D: Translate fwd/back
  * L/R: Rotate view left/right
  * Shift+U/D: Tilt up/down
  * Shift+L/R: Translate L/R
 z/Shift-Z: Adjust xsection
            thickness
On X-Section:
 Mouse clicks:
  * Left/Right: Move ruler
    (same as A/B points above)
  * Middle: Pan view up/down
  * Scroll: Change vertical scale
A: | B: | Section thickness: km | Pitch: ° | Ruler: km
