+ Instructions
Map element 
 Mouse:
    L: Drag A *or* B
    R: Drag both A *and* B

 Keyboard:
    ↑/↓: Translate fwd/back
    ←/→: Rotate view L/R
    Shift + ←/→: Translate L/R
    z/Shift+Z: Adjust slice
             thickness
Cross-section Element: 
 Mouse:
    Left/Right: Move ruler
      (same as A/B points above)
    Middle: Pan view up/down
    Scroll: Change scaling

 Keyboard:
    Shift + ↑/↓: Tilt up/down
    Space: Reset pitch & scale
